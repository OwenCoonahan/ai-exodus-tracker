Safety Teams Hit Hardest

Multiple researchers have forfeited significant equity (potentially $10M+) rather than sign non-disparagement agreements. Money isn't keeping them quiet.

The Anthropic Pipeline

Anthropic has become the destination for safety-concerned researchers. Founded by OpenAI safety team, continues to absorb departures.

Public Warnings Increasing

2024 saw more former insiders speaking publicly about concerns than any previous year. NDAs and equity clawbacks aren't stopping them.

Seniority of Departures

Not just junior researchers ‚Äî co-founders, chief scientists, and longtime executives are leaving. These people built the companies.

Timeline Acceleration

Many departures cite compressed timelines and racing dynamics. The closer they get to AGI, the more people leave.
